About the Academy

Thirteen years of building minds in Begum Pura

Founded on 6 September 2012, Al-Ilm Science Academy began as a small neighbourhood classroom in Mehmood abad Begum Pura, Lahore, with a simple conviction: that rigorous education, delivered with patience and integrity, changes the trajectory of a student's life.

In the early years, that meant one teacher, one blackboard, and a handful of Matric students who needed concepts to make sense before an exam could. What worked was never the size of the room — it was the refusal to move on until a concept actually landed.

Over the years the Academy has grown into a trusted institute for Matriculation and Intermediate students across Lahore who want more than rote learning. The subjects expanded — Pre-Medical, ICS, I.Com, F.A, and Cambridge O & A-Levels — but the method did not change.

Our name, Al-Ilm, means "the knowledge." It is both our identity and our standard.

Our Mission

To make high-quality education accessible, disciplined, and genuinely understood — not memorised.

Our Vision

To be Lahore's most trusted name in coaching, producing students who think critically and act with integrity.

Our Values

Discipline, clarity of concept, respect for the student, and honest reporting of progress to every parent.

Our Journey

From one classroom to a full campus

  1. 2012

    The Academy is founded

    Al-Ilm Science Academy opens its doors in Begum Pura, Lahore, on 6 September 2012, teaching Matric-level Science.

  2. Growth

    Programs expand

    Pre-Medical, Pre-Engineering, and ICS are added as more families ask for Intermediate-level coaching. We also introduced Arts (F.A) and Commerce (I.Com) tracks.

  3. Cambridge

    O & A-Level support begins

    Dedicated Cambridge-syllabus classes are introduced for O and A-Level students.

  4. Today

    A full educational campus

    Al-Ilm now runs structured batches across multiple core tracks, six days a week, from our dedicated campus in Mehmood abad.

Admissions

How to join Al-Ilm Science Academy

Admissions are open across the year for all programs, subject to seat availability in each batch.

  1. 1

    Visit or call the campus

    Reach us in Mehmood abad Begum Pura, Lahore, or call +92 312 4075963 during office hours to discuss the right program.

  2. 2

    Placement conversation

    A short conversation with faculty confirms the correct batch and level for the student.

  3. 3

    Registration & documents

    Complete the registration form with recent academic records and basic documents.

  4. 4

    Begin classes

    Join the next scheduled batch and receive the term's testing and reporting calendar.

Eligibility

  • Currently enrolled in, or promoted to, Class IX through Intermediate Part II
  • O or A-Level students seeking Cambridge-syllabus support
  • Seats confirmed on a first-come basis, subject to batch capacity

Documents Required

  • Most recent school/college result or report card
  • Copy of student's B-Form or CNIC
  • One recent passport-size photograph
